Associate Editors


Manhoosh Alizadeh is an Associate Professor in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at the University of California, Santa Barbara. Her research interests include learning, optimization, and control with applications to cyber-physical systems and smart grid.



Corentin Briat is a Senior Scientist at ETH-Zürich, working at the intersection of mathematics, control theory and engineering, optimization, data science, and biology. He is also an Optimization and Control engineer at Swisspods Technologies and an independent researcher.



Massimo Canale is an Associate Professor of Automatic Control at the Politecnico di Torino in Italy. He is coauthor of over 80 papers published on international journals and international conferences.



Madalena Chaves is a Senior Researcher at Inria Sophia Antipolis - Mediterranean in France. Her research is in the area of dynamical systems.



Huazhen Fang is an Associate Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the University of Kansas, where he leads the Information & Smart Systems Laboratory.



Luigi Iannelli is an Associate Professor at Department of Engineering at the University of Sannio in Italy. He held visiting researcher positions at the Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden, and the Johann Bernoulli Institute of Mathematics and Computer Science, University of Groningen, The Netherlands.



Neera Jain is an Assistant Professor in the School of Mechanical Engineering and a faculty member in the Ray W. Herrick Laboratories at Purdue University. She directs the Jain Research Laboratory, which aims to advance technologies that will have a lasting impact on society through a systems-based approach grounded in dynamic modeling and control theory.
