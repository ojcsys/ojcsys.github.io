Impact Factor Update

OJ-CSYS has been accepted for indexing in the Emerging Sources Citation Index by Clarivate, and will qualify for its initial impact factor in the June 2025 Journal Citation Reports release. The journal is also under evaluation for inclusion in SCOPUS.


New Special Section

The recurring Special Section on Intersection of Machine Learning with Control has a submission window from 15 October 2024 - 15 April 2025.

APC Discounts on Regular Papers

The first 6 regular papers published in Vol. 3 of the journal are eligible to receive a 50% discount on article processing charges (APCs). Some restrictions apply. Please contact the editorial assistant for more information.


Important Message for UC Authors

Effective January 1, 2024, the University of California will be ending open access publishing support through its agreement with IEEE. Funding for page charges will also end at that time, and UC authors who publish in IEEE journals will be responsible for covering the APCs.


New IEEE Open Access Institutional Partners

IEEE has made new open access agreements with several institutions across various countries. These agreements are designed to make it convenient for institutions and funders to support and manage costs for authors by paying for article processing charges in advance.

To see whether your institution has an open access agreement with IEEE, click here.


OJ-CSYS Included in Index

The Open Journal of Control Systems has been accepted for inclusion in the Directory of Open Access Journals. DOAJ is a community-driven index of diverse open access journals from around the world that advocates best practices and standards in open access. View the listing.
